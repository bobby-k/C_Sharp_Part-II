The Karmeli Alphabet

This is the home of the Karmeli Alphabet, which is an alternative way to write modern and classical Hebrew. The Karmeli Alphabet was developed by Michael Avinor in Haifa, Israel. We consider the Karmeli Alphabet to be superior to the traditional Assyrian alphabet.
